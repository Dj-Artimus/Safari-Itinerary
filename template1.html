Day Details

Day 1: Arrival in Nairobi

Upon arrival at Kenyatta International Airport in Nairobi, you will be warmly greeted by our representative and transferred to your luxurious hotel. Settle in and prepare for the incredible adventure that lies ahead.

The Boma Hotel Nairobi, Kenya

Day 2: Game Drive in Maasai Reserve

Rise early for an exhilarating morning game drive in the renowned Maasai Reserve, home to an astonishing array of wildlife. After a delightful lunch, embark on another captivating game drive to further explore this magnificent landscape.

Maasai Reserve Game Drive Morning & Afternoon

Day 3: Transfer to Mombasa

Following a hearty breakfast, you will be transferred to Wilson Airport for your scenic flight to Mombasa. Upon arrival, transfer to your exquisite beach resort and unwind by the tranquil ocean.

Wilson Airport Departure

Day 4: Beach Relaxation

Indulge in a day of relaxation on the pristine beaches of Mombasa, basking in the sun, sand, and sea. Optional excursions are available for those seeking additional adventures.

Mombasa Beach Relaxation & Excursions

Day 5: Departure

Enjoy a final breakfast before your transfer to Mombasa Airport for your departure flight, carrying cherished memories of your unforgettable safari adventure.

Mombasa Airport Departure
