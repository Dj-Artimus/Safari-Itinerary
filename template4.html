Embark on Your Safari Adventure

Summary

Experience the thrill of a lifetime as you journey through the heart of Africa, where the wild roams free and adventure awaits at every turn. This meticulously crafted itinerary promises an immersive experience in the breathtaking landscapes and rich wildlife of the continent, ensuring a safari that will leave you with unforgettable memories.

Day 1: Arrival in Arusha

Arrive at Kilimanjaro International Airport, transfer to hotel in Arusha. Accommodation: Mount Meru Hotel.

Day 2: Tarangire National Park

Safari in Tarangire National Park. Accommodation: Tarangire Safari Lodge.

Day 3: Ngorongoro Crater

Explore Ngorongoro Crater. Accommodation: Ngorongoro Serena Safari Lodge.

Day 4: Serengeti National Park

Safari in Serengeti National Park. Accommodation: Serengeti Safari Lodge.

Day 5: Departure

Return to Arusha and depart from Kilimanjaro International Airport.
